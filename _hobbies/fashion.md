## Style Philosophy

I believe in dressing authentically while being open to experimentation. My style evolves as I discover new designers, cultural influences, and personal preferences.

### Style Influences
- **Minimalist aesthetics**: Clean lines and quality over quantity
- **Cultural diversity**: Drawing inspiration from different cultures
- **Sustainable fashion**: Supporting ethical and eco-friendly brands
- **Vintage elements**: Incorporating timeless pieces into modern looks

## Fashion Interests

- **Designer research**: Learning about different designers and their philosophies
- **Fabric knowledge**: Understanding materials and their properties
- **Color theory**: How colors work together in outfits
- **Cultural fashion**: Exploring traditional and contemporary styles from around the world

## Shopping Philosophy

I prefer investing in quality pieces that will last rather than following fast fashion trends. This approach not only reduces environmental impact but also helps build a more thoughtful and personal wardrobe.
